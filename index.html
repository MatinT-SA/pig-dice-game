Rules of the Pig Dice Game

Objective: Be the first to reach 100 points.

Players: Two players take turns.

Gameplay: Roll a die, score points, avoid rolling a 1.

Winning: Reach or exceed 100 points.

Special Rules: Doubles add points, double 1s reset score.

Strategy: Decide when to roll and when to hold.
